## Full Description (4000 characters)

Naplet is the ultimate app for parents who want to understand and improve their baby's sleep. Developed with love by parents for parents, Naplet makes sleep tracking simple, intuitive, and useful.

**Why Choose Naplet?**

We know how challenging a baby's sleep routine can be. Between feedings, diaper changes, and naps, it's easy to lose track of time. Naplet was created to help you:

• Understand your baby's sleep patterns
• Know the best time for the next nap
• Track development over time
• Have concrete data for pediatrician visits

**Key Features:**

🌙 **Sleep Tracking**
Log naps and nighttime sleep with just one tap. Intuitive timer that works even in the background.

⏰ **Smart Wake Windows**
Receive alerts based on your baby's age. Never miss the ideal nap time again.

📊 **Detailed Statistics**
See how much your baby slept per day, week, or month. Track the evolution of their routine.

🍼 **Feeding Log**
Keep track of breastfeeding, bottles, and solid foods all in one place.

👨‍👩‍👧 **Invite Caregivers**
Share access with your partner, grandparents, or babysitter. Everyone on the same page.

🤖 **AI Sleep Assistant**
Ask questions about infant sleep and receive personalized tips based on your baby's data.

**Designed for Parents:**

• Dark interface to avoid disturbance at night
• Large buttons for one-handed use
• Works offline
• Automatic sync across devices
